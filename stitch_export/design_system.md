## Style Notes

The visual direction is Neon-Noir Minimalism: a premium, nocturnal gallery interface inspired by Japanese city pop and contemporary anime presentation. Depth is built through tonal layering and glassmorphism, with restrained electric accents and generous negative space so the artwork remains the primary visual presence.

Cards use dark slate surfaces, subtle one-pixel borders, and blue hover accents. Navigation is fixed with backdrop blur, uppercase mono labels, and a neon lime active indicator. Buttons keep a precise 4px radius, with solid blue primary actions and lime ghost secondary actions.
